As the {pad} tag does not do anything itself, it must be given content & data.
(if there is no data is assigned then it will have default 1 data occurence)

HTML RESULT 
{pad $myColor='red'}
  <b> <font color="{$myColor}"> Hallo Wereld </font> </b> <br>
{/pad}
 Hallo Wereld 
{data 'myColor'}
  ( "red", "green", "blue" ) 
{/data}
{pad data='myColor'}
  <b> <font color="{$myColor}"> Hallo Wereld </font> </b> <br>
{/pad}
 Hallo Wereld
Hallo Wereld
Hallo Wereld 
{content 'myContent'}
  <b> <font color="{$myColor}"> Hello World </font> </b> <br>
{/content}
{pad content='myContent', $myColor='purple'}
 Hello World 
{pad content='myContent', data='myColor'}
 Hello World
Hello World
Hello World

The above examples gives a sneak preview of The content tag and The data tag, 2 powerfull PAD tags.
